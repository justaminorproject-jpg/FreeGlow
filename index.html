How to use FreeGlow 🌅

Find events & meetups that actually fit your life

Getting started
1
Enter your location
Type your city, neighborhood, or zip code in the location box. FreeGlow uses this to find events and meetup groups near you.
💡 Be specific — "Atlanta, GA" works better than just "Atlanta"
2
Pick your age group
Select the age range that fits you. FreeGlow uses this to tailor event suggestions and find meetup groups with people your age.
3
Set your work schedule
Tap the days you work — they turn purple. FreeGlow automatically figures out all your upcoming free days and highlights them on the calendar.
💡 Use "Look Ahead" to search 1, 2, or 3 months out
4
Override any day
Working an extra shift? Got a random day off? Just tap that day on the calendar to flip it. Green = added as free, dashed red = marked as working.
5
Find events on your free days
Tap the purple button to search for local events happening specifically on your days off — festivals, trivia nights, classes, markets, concerts and more.
💡 Each event includes a first-timer tip to make showing up solo less scary
6
Find meetup groups near you
Tap the teal button to discover recurring community groups in your area — book clubs, hiking groups, trivia leagues, game nights, young professionals and more.
💡 Meetup groups meet regularly so you keep building connections over time
Frequently asked questions
What's the difference between Events and Meetup Groups? +
Events are one-time things happening on your specific free days — a festival, trivia night, concert, or market. Meetup Groups are recurring communities that meet regularly — book clubs, hiking groups, game nights — great for building real friendships over time.
Can I use Pick Days Manually instead? +
Yes! Tap the "Pick Days Manually" tab and tap any days on the calendar to mark them as free. Great if your schedule changes week to week or you just want to check out a specific date.
How accurate are the results? +
FreeGlow uses AI (Llama 3.3 via Groq, with Gemini as backup) to suggest realistic local events and groups. Results are AI-generated so always verify event details before heading out — check the venue's website or social media to confirm.
Is my data saved? +
No data is saved between sessions. Your schedule and location stay in your browser only and are never stored on any server. Each time you open FreeGlow you start fresh.
Why does it sometimes fail to load? +
FreeGlow uses a free AI service that occasionally hits rate limits. If it fails, wait a few seconds and try again — it automatically switches to a backup AI (Gemini) when the main one is busy.

What is FreeGlow?

FreeGlow flips the way you find things to do. Instead of browsing events and hoping they land on a day you're free, you enter your work schedule first — then FreeGlow finds what's happening on your days off.

Why We Built This

Finding your people in a new city — or just breaking out of a routine — is harder than it should be. You find something cool and then check your schedule: nope, working. FreeGlow was built so that never happens again. Your free time deserves to shine.

What It Does

Enter your schedule once and get back:

🎉 Local events on free days👥 Age-matched meetup groups📍 Location-based results💡 First-timer tips🔄 Recurring communities

Powered By

FreeGlow uses Groq (with Llama 3.3 70B) and Google Gemini as a fallback to find real, local suggestions tailored to your age group and location. Fast, free, and private — your data never gets stored.
